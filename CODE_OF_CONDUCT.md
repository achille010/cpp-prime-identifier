# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers of **cpp-prime-identifier** pledge to make participation in this project a welcoming and harassment-free experience for everyone — regardless of age, experience level, background, gender identity, nationality, race, religion, or any other personal characteristic.

This is a beginner-friendly project, and we especially encourage newcomers to C++ and algorithms. Everyone was a beginner once.

---

## Our Standards

**Positive behavior includes:**

- Being welcoming and patient with beginners
- Using inclusive and respectful language
- Giving constructive, kind feedback on code
- Acknowledging different approaches to solving the same problem
- Celebrating learning and progress, not just expertise

**Unacceptable behavior includes:**

- Mocking or belittling someone's code or skill level
- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Publishing anyone's private information without permission
- Any conduct that would make contributors feel unwelcome or unsafe

---

## Our Responsibility

The maintainer ([@achille010](https://github.com/achille010)) is responsible for upholding these standards and will take fair corrective action in response to any unacceptable behavior.

---

## Enforcement

Incidents of unacceptable behavior can be reported by contacting [@achille010](https://github.com/achille010) directly via GitHub. All reports will be handled with discretion.

Consequences for violations may include a warning, temporary suspension, or permanent removal from the project depending on severity.
